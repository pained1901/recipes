Fried-rice Recipe for Beginners

Fried rice is a versatile and delicious dish that can be customized with various ingredients. Here's a basic recipe for preparing homemade fried rice:

Ingriedients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, leftover rice works best)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as carrots, peas, corn, and bell peppers), diced
  • 1 cup cooked protein (such as diced chicken, shrimp, ham, or tofu)
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced green onions for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Ensure that all ingredients are chopped, diced, and prepared before starting to cook.
  2. Heat Oil: Heat vegetable oil or sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Scramble Eggs: Once the oil is hot, pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Allow them to cook for a minute or two without stirring to let them set slightly, then gently scramble them with a spatula until they are cooked through. Remove the scrambled eggs from the skillet and set them aside.
  4. Saute Aromatics: In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saute for 2-3 minutes until they become fragrant and slightly softened.
  5. Add Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Add Protein: Add the cooked protein of your choice (chicken, shrimp, ham, tofu, etc.) to the skillet. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the protein is heated through.
  7. Add Rice: Add the cooked rice to the skillet. Break up any clumps of rice with a spatula and stir-fry with the vegetables and protein until well combined.
  8. Seasoning: Drizzle soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) over the rice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to evenly distribute the seasoning throughout the fried rice.
  9. Incorporate Eggs: Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet with the fried rice. Gently fold them into the rice until evenly distributed.
  10. Finish and Serve: Continue to stir-fry the fried rice for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Optionally, garnish with sliced green onions for added flavor and color.
  11. Serve: Transfer the fried rice to serving plates or bowls. Enjoy hot as a delicious main dish or as a side dish with your favorite Asian-inspired meal.

  12. Feel free to customize this basic fried rice recipe by adding other ingredients such as diced ham, sliced mushrooms, or chopped pineapple. The possibilities are endless!
